## ðŸŽ¨ Title Formula

Great titles for your audience:

- **Pattern-breaking**: "Why [Common Belief] is Wrong About [Topic]"
- **Timely**: "What [Recent Event] Means for [Your Focus]"
- **How-to**: "How [Type of Company] Should Think About [Topic]"
- **Prediction**: "Why [Trend] Will/Won't [Outcome]"
- **Contrarian**: "[Hot Topic] Won't Save Banking. Here's What Will."

**Examples for your site:**
- "The Agentic Banking Layer: Why Your Bank Needs an AI Strategy"
- "Why Fintech's Next Wave Isn't Mobile-First"
- "What OpenAI's Operator Means for Banking APIs"
- "AI Agents Won't Replace Bankers. They'll Replace Banking."
